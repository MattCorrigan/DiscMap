What is DiscMap?

DiscMap is a quick and convenient way to visualize discussions and keep track of the conversation's directoin. You will be able to create discussion rooms to create a mind map of your discussion, and let others join using the uniquely generated code. DiscMap will help your discussions follow a logical development path and improve the flow of your conversation while allowing everyone to have a chance to speak.
